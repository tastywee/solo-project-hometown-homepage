Explore Boracay

Immerse in the Pinoy culture and festivities on this lively beach nestled within Philippines' archipelago.

Things to Do in Boracay for an Unforgettable Trip

Go Parasailing

Apart from getting a heart-thumping 15-minute ride, you can glimpse the island’s magnificent landscape in all its natural glory.

Enjoy a Sunset Cruise

Witness this cascading spectacle of pink and orange painting the sky through a leisurely cruise through pristine waters.

Try out Helmet Diving

Put on an oxygen-pumped helmet and stroll along the ocean floor, and meet the island’s wild marine life up close.
